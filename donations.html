Yoga is meant to be accessible to everyone

Classes Are Always Free

I believe yoga should be available to anyone who wants to practice, regardless of financial circumstances. That's why all of my classes are offered completely free of charge. There is no fee, no membership, and no obligation — just show up and move.

That said, if my teaching has brought value to your life and you'd like to give back, donations are warmly welcomed and deeply appreciated. Every contribution helps me continue offering these classes to the community.

Your support also helps fund my journey toward becoming a certified Iyengar yoga instructor. Iyengar certification is one of the most rigorous and respected credentials in the yoga world, requiring years of dedicated study and assessment. As I work toward obtaining this certification, your generosity directly supports my continued training and education.

To donate, please Venmo me at @Jessica-eifkc

Any amount is appreciated — thank you for supporting community yoga.
